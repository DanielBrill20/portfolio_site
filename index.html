Daniel is a detail-oriented, creativity-driven software developer, artist, and aspiring creative technologist. As a Mantei/Mae Scholar, he has been recognized for being in the top 1% of his class at the University of Cincinnati, where he has gained real-world experience through their co-op program. Despite his longstanding academic focus, Daniel finds deeper purpose in art and being a creative generalist. He prides himself on making inspiring and impactful work, and teaching others how to do the same. While his primary skills revolve around embedded C/C++, hardware-software communication, and Python, he is regarded for his strong willingness to learn, adopt new styles, and dive into the unknown.
